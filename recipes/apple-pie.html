Apple Pie

Ingredients

  • Pie crust
  • Apples
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Nutmeg
  • Salt
  • Lemon juice
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat over
  2. Prepare apples
  3. Roll out/trim crust
  4. Fill the pie
  5. Top the pie
  6. Brush with egg white
  7. Bake
  8. Allow pie to rest
  9. Serve
